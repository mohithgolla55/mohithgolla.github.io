Intelligent Pesticide Sprinkling System (SIH)

Developed an Intelligent Pesticide Sprinkling System as part of the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) project focused on improving modern agricultural practices through automation and smart monitoring technologies.


The system utilizes a camera-based crop monitoring mechanism to observe crop conditions in real-time and identify areas requiring pesticide application. Integrated mobile application support enables remote monitoring, system control, and improved operational efficiency for farmers.


The automated pesticide spraying mechanism was designed to ensure controlled and efficient pesticide distribution, helping to reduce unnecessary chemical usage, minimize manual effort, and improve crop protection processes in agricultural environments.


Worked as a Team Member responsible for assembly, system integration, mechanical support, and coordination during the development and testing phases of the project.
